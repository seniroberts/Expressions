

Customers Capabilities:
------------------------
1. See available inventory for rent
2. Place rental order : --> Rental Options includes:
   - Hourly Rental @ $5 per hour 
   - Daily Rental @ $15 per day
   - Weekly Rental @ $60 per week.
   - Special Offers: - Family Rental, Corporate Rental etc [apply 25% discount]


Rental Service Capabilities:
----------------------------

1. Generate and Issue Invoice
2. Publish available inventory
3. Accept & Process Rental orders 


Assumptions For V1:
--------------------

1. A single customer can choose just 1 rental option.
2. Customers can choose the number of bikes required.
